Getting started

Getting started in kickboxing is relatively simple. There are only a few items you may need to purchase before starting:

  • Workout Clothes
  • Boxing Gloves
  • Hand Wraps
  • Punching Bag

Workout Clothing

The best type of clothing to wear while kickboxing is clothing that is breathable and loose enough to allow you to move freely.

  • Loose clothing
  • Breathable clothing
  • Kickboxing is done barefoot

Boxing Gloves

Invest in high-quality boxing gloves and hand wraps to protect your hands and knuckles. Boxing gloves come in three specific varieties:

  • Heavy bag gloves
  • Boxing training gloves
  • Competitive gloves
